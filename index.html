Privacy Policy for NoteWiz

    We collect the following types of information:

If you believe that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us, and we will take appropriate steps to delete such information.

    If you wish to delete your information, you can do so by sending an email to notewizsupport@gmail.com. Please include your request in the email, and we will take appropriate measures to delete your information from our records.
